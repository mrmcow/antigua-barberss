## The 45-Minute Rule

**Real talk:** A proper haircut takes 45-60 minutes in the Caribbean. Barbers don't rush quality.

But for **true emergencies**, these barbers can deliver a solid cut in 30 minutes:

1. **Tell them it's urgent** when you walk in
2. **Bring a reference photo** - saves 10 minutes of explanation  
3. **Tip well** ($10-15 USD) - they're accommodating your emergency
4. **Keep it simple** - basic fade or trim, not experimental

## Emergency Cut Types That Work

**‚úÖ DO Request:**
- **Classic fade** (low, mid, or high)
- **Shape-up** (clean up existing cut)
- **Buzz cut** with fade sides
- **Basic trim** (take length off, clean the edges)
