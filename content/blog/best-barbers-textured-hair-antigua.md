### What Sets These Shops Apart

**The Right Tools**
- **Professional clippers** with multiple guard sizes
- **Sharp scissors** for texturing and layering  
- **Quality products** for moisture and hold

**The Experience**
- **Take their time** - no rushing through textured hair
- **Understand shrinkage** - how your hair will look when dry
- **Proper sectioning** - work with your natural growth patterns

### Communication Tips

**Show Examples**
- Photos work better than descriptions
- Instagram screenshots are perfect reference
- Point to specific details you want maintained

**Key Phrases:**
- **"Keep it natural"** - maintain your hair's texture
- **"Low fade"** vs **"high fade"** - be specific about length
- **"Shape it up"** - clean the edges without cutting length

### Post-Cut Care in Tropical Climate

**Humidity Tips:**
- Ask for **light products** that won't weigh down your hair
- **Shorter styles** tend to handle humidity better
- **Regular touch-ups** (every 2-3 weeks) keep you fresh

**Product Recommendations:**
- Local shops often stock Caribbean-appropriate products
- Many barbers can recommend what works in Antigua's climate
